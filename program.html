09:30 – 10:30 Keynote – Dr Colin Paterson (University of York)
“Safety in the Presence of Uncertainty: Understanding AI-Driven Autonomous Systems”

Abstract: AI and autonomous systems are set to revolutionise the way we interact with our world. However, integrating autonomous reasoning with cyber-physical systems challenges traditional safety assurance models. This talk explores how to argue safety in dynamic, safety-critical environments and how to assure perception, understanding, and decision-making components.

Biography: Senior Lecturer at the University of York working on safety of autonomous systems, uncertainty quantification, and assurance. Former industry professional with extensive cross-sector impact and training leadership in UKRI AI initiatives.

“Operationalisation challenges regarding UAV telemetry processing for accountability, safety, and Command & Control (C2) purposes.”
