Submission Guidelines

Submitted papers must be written in English and follow the IEEE two-column format. The papers should not exceed eight (8) pages in IEEE style.

Paper submission must be made through this Easychair link.

Accepted workshop papers will be included in the conference supplemental proceedings managed by the Conference Publishing Services (CPS).
